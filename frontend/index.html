Security & Privacy

Peer-to-Peer Transfer

Files are transferred directly from your browser to the recipient's browser. No data ever touches our servers except for initial connection signaling.

End-to-End Encryption

WebRTC DataChannels are encrypted using DTLS (Datagram Transport Layer Security) by default in all modern browsers.

No Persistence

Since we don't store your files, they can't be leaked from our servers. Once the transfer is complete and browsers are closed, no trace of the file remains on the network.

About PeerDrop

PeerDrop was built with a single goal: to make file sharing as simple and private as possible. We believe you shouldn't have to upload your private documents to a cloud server just to give them to someone in the same room.

Our technology leverages WebRTC to create a direct link between devices, ensuring maximum speed and total privacy.
